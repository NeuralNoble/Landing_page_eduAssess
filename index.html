Holistic Assessment

Get a complete picture of your child's learning progress across all worksheets they've completed. Our AI identifies knowledge gaps and provides subject and topic-wise feedback.

Knowledge Gap Identification & Question Generation

From the overall assessment, our AI finds specific concepts your child is struggling with and generates new, targeted questions to practice those weak areas.

Video Lecture Recommendations

For any weak concept, our AI suggests relevant video lessons that parents co-play for their child to help them understand the topic better.

Similar Question Generation for Mistakes

When a child gets a question wrong, AI creates another similar question for them to try, reinforcing learning right away.

Teacher-Style Annotations

When you scan a worksheet, our AI draws corrections like a human teacher would - underlining mistakes, drawing arrows or tick marks on the sheet.

Instant, Accurate AI Grading

Our AI instantly grades handwritten answers across math and science worksheets, providing detailed feedback on each question with high accuracy.
